Practical's Experience:

Positive

My experience with CS11010 is fairly positive.
The practical's worksheet was fairly challenging and well explained and structured,
it was clearly shown each worksheet was building on previously foundation.
Lecture was well explained and the example website helped me a lot
because I could analyse them and implement them to my websites.

Negative

In my opinion the best way to learn HTML are practical worksheet
because they are designed for us students to tackle new concepts of HTML
whereas lectures only gives us an idea how it works which is why I think
instead of one practical worksheet it should be two a fortnight in place of lectures.

Another problem I had was practical's and lectures not being linked, for example early
on lectures about CSS also had CSS specific practical worksheet given on the same week.
Whereas later on we was given JavaScript worksheets which was taught in previous weeks.
